# Watercolor Design System

Soft watercolor illustration style with hand-painted textures and natural warmth.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

- **Brand Personality:** Artistic, warm, gentle, expressive
- **Emotional Tone:** Inviting, calming, creative, nurturing
- **Key Characteristics:**
  - Gentle watercolor aesthetic with visible brush strokes and natural color bleeding
  - Hand-painted feel with soft edges and organic shapes
  - Warm, approachable, and artistically refined
  - Combines artistic expression with clear information delivery
  - Natural elements and organic forms throughout

---

## 3. Typography Rules

**Font Families:**
- **Headlines:** Elegant handwritten or brush script (e.g., "Dancing Script", "Great Vibes", or similar)
- **Body:** Clean rounded sans-serif or casual handwriting style (e.g., "Quicksand", "Nunito", or similar)

| Role | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height | Letter Spacing | Notes |
|------|------|------|--------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| H1 (Title) | Brush Script | 56px | Regular | 1.2 | 0.02em | Organic letterforms with natural variation |
| H2 (Section) | Brush Script | 40px | Regular | 1.3 | 0.01em | Warm, personal feeling |
| H3 (Subsection) | Brush Script | 28px | Regular | 1.4 | 0 | May appear as actual hand-painted lettering |
| Body | Rounded Sans | 18px | Regular | 1.7 | 0.01em | Readable at smaller sizes, maintains artistic cohesion |
| Caption | Rounded Sans | 14px | Regular | 1.5 | 0.02em | Functional while staying soft |
| Quote | Brush Script | 24px | Regular | 1.5 | 0.01em | Italic or flowing for artistic quotes |

**Principles:**
- Headlines have organic letterforms with natural variation
- Brush script conveys warmth and personal feeling
- Body text maintains readability while staying soft and rounded
- Typography should feel hand-crafted, not typeset
- Generous line height (1.7) for airy, breathable text

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Soft Coral (#F4A261) watercolor wash fill, Warm Charcoal text, organic rounded corners (8-12px)
- **Secondary:** Transparent with hand-painted border in Dusty Rose (#E8A0A0)
- **Ghost:** Warm Charcoal text with watercolor underline accent
- All buttons have soft, organic shapes — not perfectly geometric

### Cards & Containers
- Organic rounded corners (8-16px, not perfectly uniform)
- Watercolor wash backgrounds that don't have hard edges
- Soft color bleeds beyond container boundaries
- Subtle watercolor paper texture visible

### Inputs & Forms
- Soft rounded corners (8px)
- 1px soft border in warm gray
- Warm Charcoal text
- Focus state: Soft Coral or Sky Blue watercolor glow

### Navigation
- Soft, organic navigation items
- Active item: watercolor wash highlight behind text
- Items may have small hand-painted decorative elements

### Image Treatment
- Illustrated icons with visible brush strokes
- Natural elements: leaves, bubbles, flowers
- Color bleeds and soft edges on all visual elements
- No sharp-edged photographs — everything is painterly

### Distinctive Components
- **Watercolor Washes:** Section backgrounds with soft, bleeding color
- **Natural Elements:** Leaves, flowers, bubbles as decorative accents
- **Hand-Drawn Arrows:** Connection lines with brush stroke quality
- **Labeled Diagrams:** Watercolor fills with hand-painted labels
- **Character Illustrations:** Small expressive figures in watercolor style
- **Decorative Nature Accents:** Scattered thoughtfully throughout

## 5. Layout Principles

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 8px
- Margins: 12-15% on all sides
- Gap between elements: 24px (small), 40px (medium), 64px (large)
- Generous, flowing spacing

### Grid & Container
- Loose, organic grid — not rigidly aligned
- Elements may have slight rotation or organic positioning
- Containers have soft, bleeding edges
- Overlapping elements create depth

### Whitespace Philosophy
- Generous whitespace for breathing room
- Empty space feels like the white of watercolor paper
- Elements flow naturally with organic spacing
- Avoid cramped, grid-locked layouts

### Border Radius Scale
- Soft organic rounding (8-16px)
- Corners are not perfectly uniform
- Some elements may have entirely organic, blob-like shapes

---

## 6. Depth & Elevation

| Level | Treatment | Use |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| 0 (Flat) | Watercolor wash only | Default elements |
| 1 (Layered) | Overlapping washes with transparency | Related elements grouped |
| 2 (Highlighted) | Brighter or more saturated wash | Emphasized sections |

**Shadow Philosophy:** Watercolor style does not use digital drop shadows. Depth is created through overlapping transparent washes, color intensity, and organic layering. Elements appear to be painted on the same surface rather than floating above it. The transparency and bleeding of watercolor pigments naturally create depth through color interaction.

---

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Allow color to bleed beyond sharp edges
- Use visible brush stroke textures
- Create soft, organic shapes
- Include hand-drawn quality in all elements
- Maintain warm, inviting color palette
- Use natural elements as decorative accents
- Layer transparent washes for depth
- Allow imperfection in shapes and edges

### Don't
- Use sharp geometric shapes or hard edges
- Create digital precision or pixel-perfect alignment
- Use cold or stark colors
- Add photographic elements
- Apply drop shadows or digital elevation
- Use angular or aggressive shapes
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos
- Create rigid, grid-locked layouts

---

## 8. Responsive Behavior

| Breakpoint | Width | Behavior |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Desktop | >= 1280px | Full watercolor layouts, layered elements, decorative accents |
| Tablet | 768px - 1279px | Simplified layouts, fewer decorative elements |
| Mobile | < 768px | Single column, larger text, minimal decoration |

**Touch Targets:** Minimum 48x48px for interactive elements

**Collapsing Strategy:**
- Side-by-side content stacks vertically
- Layered elements may simplify to single layer
- Decorative accents reduce in number on smaller screens
- Maintain soft, organic feel at all sizes

**Image Behavior:**
- Illustrations scale proportionally
- Watercolor textures remain visible at all sizes
- Text remains legible — prioritize readability

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

### Quick Color Reference
- Background: #FAF8F0 (Warm Off-White) or #FFF9E6 (Soft Cream)
- Primary Text: #3D3D3D (Warm Charcoal)
- Primary Accent: #F4A261 (Soft Coral)
- Secondary Accent: #E8A0A0 (Dusty Rose)
- Nature: #87A96B (Sage Green)
- Calm/Water: #7EC8E3 (Sky Blue)
- Creativity: #C5B4E3 (Soft Lavender)
- Wash: #FFF3C4 (Pale Yellow)

### Example Component Prompts

1. **Watercolor Title Slide:** "Create a title slide with warm off-white (#FAF8F0) background and subtle watercolor paper texture. Title in elegant brush script (Warm Charcoal #3D3D3D). A Soft Coral (#F4A261) watercolor wash behind the title that bleeds softly beyond the text edges. Small painted leaf accents in Sage Green (#87A96B) in the corners."

2. **Nature-Themed Card:** "Design a content card with organic rounded corners (12px). Background is a Pale Yellow (#FFF3C4) watercolor wash with soft edges. Content text in rounded sans-serif. A small hand-painted flower illustration in Dusty Rose (#E8A0A0) in the corner. The wash bleeds slightly beyond the card boundary."

3. **Process Flow with Washes:** "Illustrate a 3-step process with each step in a soft watercolor wash circle (Soft Coral, Sky Blue, Sage Green). Circles have organic, imperfect edges. Steps connected by hand-painted arrows with brush stroke texture. Labels below in rounded sans-serif. Background: Soft Cream (#FFF9E6)."

4. **Quote with Watercolor Highlight:** "Present a quote with the text in elegant brush script. Key words have a Soft Coral (#F4A261) watercolor highlight wash behind them that bleeds softly. Small decorative bubbles in Sky Blue (#7EC8E3) scattered around. Warm off-white background."

5. **Lifestyle Section:** "Create a lifestyle content section with a large watercolor wash background in Pale Yellow (#FFF3C4). Content cards with organic shapes and soft edges. Decorative leaf illustrations in Sage Green (#87A96B) along the edges. Text in rounded sans-serif for readability."

6. **Creative Workshop Layout:** "Design a workshop announcement with an artistic layout. Title in large brush script. A Soft Lavender (#C5B4E3) watercolor splash as a decorative element. Details in rounded sans-serif. Organic container shapes with bleeding color edges. Warm off-white background with paper texture."

### Iteration Guide
1. Start with warm off-white or soft cream background with paper texture
2. Add content with brush script headlines and rounded sans-serif body
3. Create organic container shapes with soft, bleeding edges
4. Apply watercolor washes as section backgrounds — let colors bleed
5. Add hand-painted decorative elements (leaves, flowers, bubbles)
6. Use visible brush stroke textures on all illustrated elements
7. Layer transparent washes for depth and color interaction
8. Review for hard edges — soften any sharp boundaries
