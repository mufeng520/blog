# Morandi Journal Design System

Hand-drawn doodle illustration with warm Morandi color tones and cozy bullet journal aesthetic.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

**Brand Personality:**
- Warm, personal, and intimately handcrafted
- Cozy, approachable, and creatively free
- Nostalgic yet contemporary
- Artistic and slightly imperfect by design

**Emotional Description:**
The Morandi Journal style feels like opening a beloved friend's sketchbook — pages filled with thoughtful doodles, washi tape remnants, and handwritten notes in warm, muted colors. It evokes the comfort of a rainy afternoon with tea and colored pens, the satisfaction of organizing thoughts by hand, and the charm of intentional imperfection. The aesthetic is deeply human, rejecting digital coldness in favor of tactile warmth.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Hand-drawn doodle illustrations with organic, slightly imperfect ink lines
- Washi tape strip decorations with torn paper edges
- Rounded card containers with wobbly, hand-drawn borders
- Hand-drawn rulers, scales, and progress bars
- Smiley and frowny faces as quality markers
- Dotted line frames around sections
- Connecting arrows and dotted lines between modules
- Corner decorations: tiny houses, stars, sparkles, clouds
- Wavy line dividers between sections
- Callout bubbles for tips and annotations
- Magnifying glass icons for identification tips
- Thumbs up/down icons in hand-drawn style

## 3. Typography Rules

**Font Family:**
- Main title: Hand-lettered calligraphy (e.g., "Caveat", "Dancing Script", "Sacramento")
- Headers: Handwritten sans-serif (e.g., "Patrick Hand", "Comic Neue", "Kalam")
- Body: Neat handwritten print (e.g., "Architect's Daughter", "Kalam", "Indie Flower")
- Annotations: Same handwritten family, smaller size

**Hierarchy Table:**

| Role | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height | Letter Spacing | Notes |
|------|------|------|--------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| Main Title | Calligraphy | 32-40px | 700 | 1.2 | 0em | With decorative flourishes |
| Module Header | Handwritten Sans | 16-20px | 700 | 1.3 | 0.01em | White on dark teal badge |
| Body Text | Handwritten Print | 13-15px | 400 | 1.5 | 0em | Neat but organic |
| Numbers | Handwritten Sans | 16-20px | 700 | 1.2 | 0em | Terracotta color, slightly larger |
| Annotations | Handwritten Sans | 10-12px | 400 | 1.4 | 0.01em | Teal or charcoal |
| Emoji Quality | System Emoji | 14-16px | — | 1.0 | 0em | Or hand-drawn versions |
| Callout Text | Handwritten Sans | 12px | 400 | 1.3 | 0em | Inside bubble |

**Typography Principles:**
- Imperfect, organic line weight variation throughout
- No perfectly straight lines or geometric precision
- Decorative curls and flourishes on title letters
- Slightly rotated text blocks acceptable
- Uneven baselines add to handmade charm

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Rounded rectangle with hand-drawn border, teal fill, white text
- **Secondary:** Rounded rectangle with wobbly border, transparent fill, charcoal text
- **Icon Button:** Small circle with hand-drawn icon inside
- **Tab:** Rounded badge with handwritten label

### Cards & Containers
- **Rounded Card:** Hand-drawn rounded rectangle border, subtle fill, corner doodle
- **Dotted Frame:** Border made of hand-drawn dots or small circles
- **Callout Bubble:** Cloud or organic blob shape with tail, pale yellow fill
- **Washi Tape Section:** Diagonal tape strip marking section boundary

### Inputs & Forms
- **Text Input:** Hand-drawn underline, no box
- **Checkbox:** Hand-drawn square with checkmark
- **Rating:** Row of hand-drawn stars or smiley faces

### Navigation
- **Section Nav:** Horizontal row of rounded badges
- **Page Indicator:** Small dots or stars
- **Back/Forward:** Hand-drawn arrows

### Image Treatment
- All visuals are hand-drawn doodle illustrations
- Organic, slightly imperfect ink lines
- Visible line weight variation
- No digital precision or vector perfection

### Distinctive Components
- **Rounded Card:** Rounded rectangle with slightly irregular hand-drawn border, 2px teal, corner doodle (star, house, cloud)
- **Washi Tape Strip:** Diagonal or horizontal strip with torn edges, beige and brown diagonal stripes, 90% opacity, slight rotation (±2 degrees)
- **Dotted Frame:** Border of hand-drawn dots or small circles in teal or charcoal
- **Hand-Drawn Ruler:** Sketchy line with tick marks of varying heights, handwritten terracotta numbers
- **Quality Indicator:** Happy (smiley + checkmark in teal), Neutral (neutral face in gray), Unhappy (frowny + cross in terracotta)
- **Connecting Arrow:** Hand-drawn curved arrow with slight wobble, heart-shaped or triangular head
- **Callout Bubble:** Cloud or organic blob with tail, pale yellow fill, charcoal border
- **Wavy Divider:** Hand-drawn wavy horizontal line with small dots or stars
- **Magnifying Glass Icon:** Hand-drawn circle with handle, charcoal outline, pale yellow fill
- **Thumbs Up/Down:** Hand-drawn cartoon hands, teal for up, terracotta for down
- **Corner Decoration:** Tiny houses, stars, sparkles, clouds at card corners
- **Progress Bar:** Hand-drawn ruler-style bar with emoji quality indicators

## 5. Layout Principles

**Spacing System:**
- Base unit: 8px (loose, organic)
- Card padding: 20-28px
- Section gap: 32-48px
- Element gap: 12-16px
- Margin: 24-32px, uneven preferred

**Grid & Container:**
- Loose, organic flow — no strict grid
- Sections separated by wavy dividers or washi tape
- Elements may slightly overlap for layered feel
- Uneven margins — wider on one side for handmade feel
- Maximum content width: 800px for cozy reading

**Whitespace Philosophy:**
- Generous and uneven whitespace mimics natural page use
- White space is part of the handmade charm
- No rigid alignment — casual, journal-like flow
- Layering (tape over paper, stickers over text) adds depth

**Border Radius Scale:**
- Organic: Hand-drawn curves (no perfect circles)
- Rounded: 8-16px for cards (slightly irregular)
- Circular: Hand-drawn circles for icons and badges

## 6. Depth & Elevation

**Elevation Table:**

| Level | Treatment | Use |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Paper Background | Flat with texture | Warm cream with grain |
| Washi Tape | Slight overlap | Tape over paper edges |
| Cards & Frames | Subtle ink shadow | `rgba(74,69,64,0.1)` under major elements |
| Stickers & Icons | Slight rotation | Angled placement |
| Overlays | Soft shadow | Callout bubbles, tooltips |

**Shadow Philosophy:**
No digital shadows or gradients. Depth is created through:
- Washi tape overlapping page edges
- Slightly rotated elements
- Subtle ink shadow under major cards
- Layering of elements (tape over paper, stickers over text)
- Paper grain texture visible throughout

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use hand-drawn, organic lines with visible weight variation
- Add washi tape, stickers, and corner doodles for warmth
- Keep the color palette muted and warm — Morandi tones
- Use rounded, imperfect shapes throughout
- Include smiley faces and hand-drawn quality indicators
- Let elements be slightly rotated or offset
- Use warm cream paper background, never pure white
- Add flourishes and decorative curls to titles
- Include connecting arrows and dotted lines between modules
- Maintain a cozy, personal, sketchbook feel

### Don'ts
- Don't use flat vector icons or system emoji — draw doodle versions
- Don't create perfectly geometric or aligned shapes
- Don't use stock illustration style or digital precision
- Don't apply a strict grid layout
- Don't use pure white background — always warm cream
- Don't introduce corporate or clinical aesthetics
- Don't use sharp corners or perfect circles
- Don't use gradients or digital shadows
- Don't use thin, precise lines — keep them organic
- Don't forget the paper texture

## 8. Responsive Behavior

**Breakpoints:**

| Breakpoint | Width | Behavior |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Mobile | < 640px | Vertical scroll, washi tape as horizontal dividers, doodles simplify |
| Tablet | 640-1024px | Single page view, decorations scale, handwriting legible |
| Desktop | > 1024px | Full journal spread, side annotations in margins, all decorations |

**Touch Targets:**
- Interactive doodles: 44x44px minimum
- Cards and buttons: 48px height minimum
- Navigation badges: 44x44px minimum

**Collapsing Strategy:**
- Journal sections stack vertically on mobile
- Side annotations move inline on smaller screens
- Decorations simplify but remain present
- Handwriting size increases slightly on mobile

**Image Behavior:**
- Doodle illustrations scale cleanly
- Paper texture remains visible at all sizes
- Washi tape strips adapt to container width
- Corner decorations may hide on very small screens
