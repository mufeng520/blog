# Soft Analog Future Editorial Style

## Overview
A sophisticated editorial aesthetic blending analog film photography warmth with futuristic minimalism. Features soft focus, grain textures, and elegant typography with a forward-looking sensibility. Ideal for fashion editorials, luxury branding, and cultural publications.

## Visual Characteristics
- **Photography Style**: Soft focus with shallow depth of field, film grain, light leaks
- **Color Palette**: Warm muted tones (cream, beige, soft pink, pale gold) with cool futuristic accents
- **Typography**: Elegant serif fonts for headlines, clean sans-serif for body; generous letter-spacing
- **Layout**: Asymmetrical editorial grid with generous white space
- **Textures**: Film grain, paper texture, subtle halftone dots
- **Decorative**: Thin geometric lines, abstract shapes, holographic foil accents

## Composition Rules
- Subject positioned with intentional negative space
- Layered elements: photograph + geometric overlays + text
- Grid-based but with deliberate breaks for visual interest
- Soft edges and gradients blending analog warmth with digital precision
- Hierarchical text with dramatic size contrasts

## Prompt Template
```
Create a soft analog future editorial layout featuring {SUBJECT}.

Photography: Soft-focus film photography with shallow depth of field, visible grain, and subtle light leaks.

Subject: {SUBJECT} styled with {WARDROBE_STYLE}, positioned elegantly with intentional negative space.

Typography: Elegant serif headline "{MAIN_TEXT}" with generous letter-spacing. Clean sans-serif body text "{SECONDARY_TEXT}" below.

Color Palette: Warm muted tones (cream, beige, soft pink) with cool futuristic accents (pale blue, silver).

Textures: Apply film grain, paper texture, and subtle halftone dot patterns.

Decorative: Add thin geometric lines, abstract shapes, and holographic foil accents.

Layout: Asymmetrical editorial grid with generous white space and layered elements.

Mood: Sophisticated, nostalgic yet forward-looking, dreamy, editorial, luxurious.
```

## Variable Substitutions
- `{SUBJECT}`: Main subject (person, product, or scene)
- `{WARDROBE_STYLE}`: Fashion styling description
- `{MAIN_TEXT}`: Editorial headline
- `{SECONDARY_TEXT}`: Subheadline or body text
- `{LOCATION}`: Setting (studio, urban loft, natural landscape)
- `{BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}`: Props or environmental details

## Example Usage
```
SUBJECT: a young model
WARDROBE_STYLE: oversized beige blazer and flowing silk pants
MAIN_TEXT: "The Future is Soft"
SECONDARY_TEXT: "How analog warmth is reshaping digital culture"
LOCATION: minimalist studio with natural light
BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS: sheer curtains, potted plants, geometric sculptures
```

## Aspect Ratio Adaptations
- **1:1 (Square)**: Centered subject, circular crop feel, Instagram editorial
- **16:9 (Landscape)**: Wide cinematic composition, subject off-center, magazine spread
- **9:16 (Portrait)**: Full-body fashion shot, vertical text alignment, story format
- **4:3 (Standard)**: Classic editorial layout, balanced proportions, print-ready
