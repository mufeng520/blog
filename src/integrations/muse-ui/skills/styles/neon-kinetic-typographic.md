# Neon Kinetic Typographic Style

## Overview
A high-energy, futuristic typographic style featuring glowing neon text with motion blur and kinetic energy. Inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics, nightclub signage, and racing culture. Perfect for announcements, event posters, and tech-forward branding.

## Visual Characteristics
- **Typography**: Bold, condensed sans-serif fonts with extreme weight; text appears to be in motion
- **Color Palette**: Deep blacks with electric neon accents (hot pink, cyan, lime green, purple)
- **Effects**: Motion blur, light trails, glow halos, chromatic aberration
- **Lines**: Speed lines, streaks, and light beams radiating from text
- **Texture**: Digital glitch artifacts, scan lines, subtle noise
- **Background**: Dark void or urban nightscape with bokeh lights

## Composition Rules
- Text dominates the composition, often at extreme angles
- Layered text with multiple copies offset to create motion trails
- Neon glow creates depth and atmosphere
- Diagonal composition suggesting speed and movement
- Contrast between dark background and bright text is extreme

## Prompt Template
```
Create a neon kinetic typographic poster for {SUBJECT}.

Style: Cyberpunk neon with motion blur, light trails, and high-energy kinetic typography.

Main Text: "{MAIN_TEXT}" in bold, condensed sans-serif with extreme glow effects and motion trails.

Secondary Text: "{SECONDARY_TEXT}" in smaller neon text with chromatic aberration.

Effects: Add speed lines, light beams, digital glitch artifacts, and scan lines.

Color Palette: Deep black background with electric {ACCENT_COLOR} neon glow (hot pink, cyan, lime green, or purple).

Background: Dark urban nightscape with distant bokeh lights or pure digital void.

Mood: High-energy, futuristic, intense, electrifying, fast-paced.
```

## Variable Substitutions
- `{SUBJECT}`: Event, product, or theme
- `{MAIN_TEXT}`: Primary bold headline
- `{SECONDARY_TEXT}`: Date, venue, or tagline
- `{ACCENT_COLOR}`: Neon accent color preference
- `{LOCATION}`: Urban setting or digital environment

## Example Usage
```
SUBJECT: a music festival
MAIN_TEXT: "NEON NIGHTS"
SECONDARY_TEXT: "July 15-17 • Downtown"
ACCENT_COLOR: hot pink and cyan
LOCATION: futuristic city skyline
```

## Aspect Ratio Adaptations
- **1:1 (Square)**: Centered explosive text, radial energy lines, social media post
- **16:9 (Landscape)**: Horizontal motion trails, wide text sweep, banner format
- **9:16 (Portrait)**: Vertical text stack, falling light beams, story format
- **4:3 (Standard)**: Balanced composition with text at dynamic angle
