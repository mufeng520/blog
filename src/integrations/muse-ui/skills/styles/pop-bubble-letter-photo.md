# Pop Bubble Letter Photo Poster Style

## Overview
A vibrant, nostalgic 2000s-era aesthetic combining bold bubble letters with photo cutouts and playful decorative elements. Features thick outlines, bright colors, and a scrapbook-like collage feel. Perfect for party invitations, celebration announcements, and retro-themed social content.

## Visual Characteristics
- **Typography**: Large, rounded bubble letters with thick black outlines and 3D drop shadows
- **Photo Treatment**: Cutout photos with white borders, slightly tilted, sticker-like appearance
- **Color Palette**: Bright, saturated rainbow colors (hot pink, lime green, electric blue, sunny yellow)
- **Decorative Elements**: Stars, hearts, music notes, peace signs, butterflies, glitter sparkles
- **Textures**: Glossy plastic shine, glitter effects, holographic accents
- **Background**: Gradient rainbow, checkerboard pattern, or solid bright color with subtle texture

## Composition Rules
- Large bubble text dominates the upper portion
- Photo cutouts scattered below and around text, overlapping slightly
- Decorative elements fill gaps and create visual rhythm
- Slight tilt and rotation on elements for playful energy
- White outlines around photos and text for pop-out effect

## Prompt Template
```
Create a pop bubble letter photo poster for {SUBJECT}.

Style: 2000s Y2K aesthetic with bold bubble letters, photo cutouts, and playful decorative elements.

Main Text: "{MAIN_TEXT}" in large, rounded bubble letters with thick black outlines, 3D drop shadows, and glossy plastic shine.

Photo Elements: Add cutout photos of {SUBJECT} with white borders, slightly tilted and overlapping.

Decorative: Scatter stars, hearts, music notes, peace signs, butterflies, and glitter sparkles throughout.

Color Palette: Bright saturated rainbow colors — hot pink, lime green, electric blue, sunny yellow, orange.

Textures: Apply glossy plastic shine, glitter effects, and holographic accents.

Background: Gradient rainbow or checkerboard pattern with subtle texture.

Mood: Celebratory, nostalgic, fun, energetic, youthful, party vibes.
```

## Variable Substitutions
- `{SUBJECT}`: Person, event, or theme
- `{MAIN_TEXT}`: Celebration headline
- `{SECONDARY_TEXT}`: Date, location, or tagline
- `{PRODUCT_OR_PROP}`: Party props or decorative items
- `{ACCENT_SYMBOL}`: Decorative symbol (star, heart, butterfly)

## Example Usage
```
SUBJECT: a birthday party
MAIN_TEXT: "HAPPY BIRTHDAY!"
SECONDARY_TEXT: "Sarah's Sweet 16"
PRODUCT_OR_PROP: balloons, confetti, cake
ACCENT_SYMBOL: stars and hearts
```

## Aspect Ratio Adaptations
- **1:1 (Square)**: Centered bubble text, circular photo arrangement, social media post
- **16:9 (Landscape)**: Wide banner with text spanning width, photos below, event flyer
- **9:16 (Portrait)**: Vertical poster, stacked bubble letters, photo collage, story format
- **4:3 (Standard)**: Classic party invitation layout, balanced text and photos
