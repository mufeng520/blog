# Vintage Design System

Vintage aged-paper aesthetic for historical and expedition-style presentations.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

- **Brand Personality:** Nostalgic, adventurous, scholarly, and timelessly elegant
- **Emotional Description:** Nostalgic vintage aesthetic with aged paper textures and historical document styling
- **Key Characteristics:**
  - Explorer's journals, antique maps, and museum exhibits aesthetic
  - Rich warm tones with weathered textures
  - Evokes discovery, heritage, and timeless knowledge
  - Layered collage compositions
  - Period-appropriate visual language
  - Warm sepia-toned palette throughout

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Aged Parchment | #F5E6D3 | Primary background |
| **Secondary** | Sepia Cream | #FFF8DC | Secondary areas, cards |
| **Primary Text** | Dark Brown | #3D2914 | Headlines, body text |
| **Secondary Text** | Medium Brown | #6B4423 | Annotations, captions |
| **Accent 1** | Forest Green | #2D5A3D | Maps, nature elements |
| **Accent 2** | Navy Blue | #1E3A5F | Ocean, route lines, ink |
| **Accent 3** | Burgundy | #722F37 | Emphasis, borders, stamps |
| **Accent 4** | Gold | #C9A227 | Highlights, compass, decorative |
| **Ink** | Sepia Black | #3D3D3D | Fine details, handwriting |
| **Surface** | Warm Tan | #E8D5B7 | Content containers, map backgrounds |

**Color Philosophy:** Warm sepia-toned palette evoking aged paper and historical documents. Colors are muted and earthy — no bright or neon tones. Gold accents provide elegance. Forest green and navy blue for map and nature elements.

---

## 3. Typography Rules

**Font Family:**
- **Headlines:** Classic serif with historical character (Garamond, Baskerville, Cinzel)
- **Labels:** Condensed serif or clean sans-serif for map labels
- **Body:** Readable serif (Georgia, Garamond) for longer text
- **Handwritten:** Script font for annotations and personal notes

**Hierarchy Table:**

| Role | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height | Letter Spacing | Notes |
|------|------|------|--------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| Title | Classic Serif | 40-56px | 700 | 1.1 | 0.02em | Elegant, timeless, may have flourishes |
| Chapter Head | Classic Serif | 28-36px | 600 | 1.2 | 0.01em | Period-appropriate styling |
| Subhead | Serif | 20-24px | 600 | 1.3 | 0 | Clear hierarchy |
| Body | Readable Serif | 16px | 400 | 1.7 | 0 | Traditional book typography |
| Caption | Serif | 12-14px | 400 | 1.4 | 0.01em | Image captions, metadata |
| Map Label | Condensed Serif | 11-13px | 500 | 1.2 | 0.03em | Period-appropriate, consistent |
| Handwritten | Script | 14-18px | 400 | 1.4 | 0 | Annotations, personal notes |

**Principles:**
- Classic serif with historical character for headlines
- Elegant, authoritative, timeless feel
- Traditional book typography for body text
- Handwritten-style for annotations and personal touches
- Period-appropriate styling throughout

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Burgundy (#722F37) background, Sepia Cream text, 4px border-radius, classic serif font
- **Secondary:** Transparent with 2px Dark Brown border, Dark Brown text, 4px border-radius
- **Action:** Gold (#C9A227) background, Dark Brown text, with decorative motif

### Cards & Containers
- Warm Tan (#E8D5B7) or Sepia Cream (#FFF8DC) background
- Subtle aged texture overlay
- 1px Medium Brown (#6B4423) border
- 4-8px border-radius
- Padding 24-32px
- Optional worn edge effect

### Inputs & Forms
- Aged Parchment (#F5E6D3) background
- 1px Medium Brown border
- 4px border-radius
- Focus: Gold (#C9A227) border
- Labels: Classic serif, 14px, Dark Brown

### Navigation
- Aged Parchment background, bottom border in Medium Brown
- Classic serif, 14px
- Active: Burgundy underline or Gold highlight

### Image Treatment
- Antique maps with route lines and landmarks
- Compass roses and nautical elements
- Expedition ship or vehicle illustrations
- Specimen drawings (flora, fauna, fossils)
- Vintage photograph-style image frames
- Handwritten-style annotations

### Distinctive Components
- **Map Card:** Aged paper background with route lines, landmarks, and compass rose
- **Specimen Frame:** Vintage frame with specimen drawing and handwritten label
- **Journal Entry:** Lined paper background with handwritten text and ink splatters
- **Expedition Badge:** Circular badge with rope/leather border and expedition name
- **Photo Frame:** Vintage photograph frame with sepia-toned image
- **Compass Element:** Decorative compass rose with Gold accents

---

## 5. Layout Principles

**Spacing System:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Section padding: 48-64px vertical
- Content max-width: 1000px centered
- Generous spacing for vintage elegance

**Grid & Container:**
- Asymmetric, collage-like layouts
- Layered compositions with overlapping elements
- Map-based layouts with irregular regions
- Journal-style flowing layouts

**Whitespace Philosophy:**
- Warm, inviting whitespace
- Elements can overlap in collage style
- Room for textures and decorative elements
- Unstructured, organic feel

**Border Radius Scale:**
- Small (buttons, tags): 4px
- Medium (cards, frames): 4-8px
- Large (feature sections): 8-12px
- Worn/irregular edges preferred for authenticity

---

## 6. Depth & Elevation

| Level | Treatment | Use |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| 0 | Flat, textured | Background paper texture |
| 1 | Subtle shadow | Cards, frames with slight lift |
| 2 | Layered collage | Overlapping elements with shadow |
| 3 | Elevated frame | Vintage frames with depth |
| 4 | Decorative overlay | Stamps, wax seals, ribbons |

**Shadow Philosophy:** Warm, brown-tinted shadows (not gray). Shadows are soft and subtle, evoking natural light on aged paper. Layered collage compositions create depth through overlap and shadow. Decorative elements like stamps and wax seals add tactile depth.

---

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Apply consistent aged texture throughout
- Use period-appropriate visual language
- Include map and journey elements where relevant
- Create layered collage compositions
- Maintain warm sepia-toned palette
- Add handwritten-style annotations
- Use rope, leather, and brass decorative motifs

### Don't
- Use modern digital styling
- Create crisp clean edges
- Use cold or bright colors
- Add contemporary elements
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos
- Use sans-serif for headlines
- Create sterile or clinical layouts

---

## 8. Responsive Behavior

| Breakpoint | Width | Behavior |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Mobile | < 640px | Single column, collage simplifies, headline 28px |
| Tablet | 640-1024px | 2-column layout, maps at 80% scale, headline 36px |
| Desktop | > 1024px | Full collage layout, maps at full detail, headline 40-56px |

**Touch Targets:** Minimum 48px for interactive elements

**Collapsing Strategy:**
- Collage layouts simplify to stacked elements
- Maps become scrollable or simplified
- Decorative elements reduce in number
- Layered elements stack with spacing

**Image Behavior:**
- Antique maps maintain detail at all sizes
- Specimen drawings scale proportionally
- Vintage frames remain prominent
- Textures tile seamlessly

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

### Quick Color Reference
- Background: #F5E6D3 (Aged Parchment) / #FFF8DC (Sepia Cream)
- Primary Text: #3D2914 (Dark Brown)
- Accent: #2D5A3D (Forest Green), #1E3A5F (Navy Blue), #C9A227 (Gold)
- Ink: #3D3D3D (Sepia Black)

### Example Component Prompts

1. **Expedition Map:** "Aged Parchment (#F5E6D3) background with heavy paper texture. Antique map with route lines in Navy Blue (#1E3A5F). Landmark icons in Forest Green (#2D5A3D). Compass rose in Gold (#C9A227) in corner. Handwritten annotations in Sepia Black. Worn edges."

2. **Journal Entry:** "Lined paper background with Aged Parchment color. Handwritten text in script font (16px, Sepia Black). Ink splatter decorations. Small specimen drawing in corner with handwritten label. Burgundy (#722F37) ribbon bookmark."

3. **Specimen Card:** "Warm Tan (#E8D5B7) card with vintage frame border. Central specimen illustration (flora or fauna) in detailed line art. Handwritten label below in script font. Gold (#C9A227) corner decorations. Subtle paper texture."

4. **Expedition Timeline:** "Horizontal timeline on Aged Parchment background. Events marked with Gold (#C9A227) dots connected by Navy Blue (#1E3A5F) route line. Each event has a date in classic serif (14px, Burgundy #722F37) and description in body serif (16px, Dark Brown)."

5. **Heritage Hero:** "Layered collage composition. Background: faded antique map. Middle: vintage photograph in ornate frame. Foreground: expedition title in classic serif (48px, Dark Brown) with Gold (#C9A227) decorative underline. Handwritten subtitle in script."

6. **Discovery Badge:** "Circular badge with rope border in Medium Brown (#6B4423). Center: expedition icon in Gold (#C9A227). Text around edge in condensed serif (12px, all-caps). Aged Parchment background. Subtle shadow for depth."

### Iteration Guide

1. **Start with texture:** Apply aged paper texture to the background first.
2. **Choose warm tones:** Ensure all colors are warm and muted, no cold/bright colors.
3. **Add historical elements:** Include maps, compass roses, specimen drawings, or vintage frames.
4. **Layer the composition:** Create depth through overlapping collage elements.
5. **Add handwritten touches:** Include script annotations and personal notes.
6. **Apply worn effects:** Add subtle creases, stains, or worn edges for authenticity.
