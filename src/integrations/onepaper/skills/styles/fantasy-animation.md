# Fantasy Animation

Whimsical hand-drawn animation style inspired by classic fantasy illustration and storybook aesthetics.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

- **Brand Personality:** Enchanting, warm, nostalgic, and emotionally engaging
- **Emotional Description:** Charming hand-drawn aesthetic reminiscent of classic Disney, Studio Ghibli, or European storybook illustration
- **Key Characteristics:**
  - Soft, painterly textures with warm, inviting colors
  - Friendly characters and magical elements
  - Storybook layouts with layered depth
  - Gentle watercolor washes and soft brush strokes
  - Enchanting, nostalgic atmosphere
  - Emotionally resonant and family-friendly

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Soft Sky Blue | #E8F4FC | Primary background |

**Color Philosophy:** Warm, soft, and inviting palette. Colors feel hand-mixed like watercolor paints. No harsh or neon tones. Every color contributes to the enchanting atmosphere.

---

## 3. Typography Rules

**Font Family:**
- **Headlines:** Whimsical serif (Cinzel, Fairy Tale, or decorative hand-lettered style)
- **Body:** Rounded sans-serif (Nunito, Quicksand) or casual handwritten style

**Hierarchy Table:**

| Role | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height | Letter Spacing | Notes |
|------|------|------|--------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| Story Title | Whimsical Serif | 48-64px | 700 | 1.1 | 0.02em | Slight curvature, organic feel |
| Chapter Head | Whimsical Serif | 32-40px | 600 | 1.2 | 0.01em | Decorative flourishes optional |
| Subhead | Rounded Sans | 20-24px | 600 | 1.3 | 0 | Friendly, readable |
| Body | Rounded Sans | 16px | 400 | 1.7 | 0 | Comfortable, storybook feel |
| Caption | Handwritten | 12-14px | 400 | 1.4 | 0.01em | Personal, informal |
| Magic Text | Whimsical Serif | 24-32px | 700 | 1.2 | 0.03em | Golden color, sparkle effect |

**Principles:**
- Headlines have slight curvature and organic feel
- Maintain storybook aesthetic while staying legible
- Friendly, approachable character throughout
- Magic/special text can have decorative treatment

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Golden Yellow (#F4D03F) background, Deep Forest text, 16px border-radius, soft shadow
- **Secondary:** Transparent with 2px Sage Green border, Sage Green text, 16px border-radius
- **Magic:** Gradient from Golden Yellow to Rose Pink, white text, rounded pill shape, sparkle decoration

### Cards & Containers
- Warm Cream (#FFF8E7) or Soft Lavender (#F0E6FA) background
- 16-24px border-radius (soft, rounded corners)
- Soft shadow (0 4px 12px rgba(45,90,61,0.1))
- Decorative corner elements (leaves, stars, swirls)
- Padding 24-32px

### Inputs & Forms
- Soft Sky Blue (#E8F4FC) background
- 2px border in Sage Green (#87A96B)
- 12px border-radius
- Focus: Golden Yellow border glow
- Labels: Warm Brown, handwritten style

### Navigation
- Transparent or soft gradient background
- Rounded sans-serif, 14px
- Active: Golden Yellow underline or background
- Nav items feel like storybook chapter titles

### Image Treatment
- Soft edges, painterly textures
- Watercolor wash effects
- Gentle shadows and soft highlights
- Layered depth with foreground/background elements

### Distinctive Components
- **Character Card:** Rounded container with character illustration, name in whimsical font, decorative border
- **Magic Item:** Glowing container with sparkle decorations, golden accents
- **Storybook Page:** Cream background with decorative corner elements, text in columns
- **Scroll Container:** Rolled parchment appearance with ribbon decoration
- **Floating Element:** Soft shadow, gentle float animation implied

## 5. Layout Principles

**Spacing System:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Section padding: 48-64px vertical
- Content max-width: 1000px centered
- Generous spacing between elements for breathing room

**Grid & Container:**
- Asymmetric, organic layouts (not rigid grids)
- Layered compositions with overlapping elements
- Panoramic horizontal compositions work well
- Depth through foreground/background layering

**Whitespace Philosophy:**
- Soft, flowing whitespace
- Elements can overlap gently
- Create depth through layering, not rigid separation
- Breathing room around characters and focal points

**Border Radius Scale:**
- Small (buttons, tags): 12-16px
- Medium (cards, containers): 16-24px
- Large (feature sections): 24-32px
- Organic shapes preferred over perfect circles/rectangles

---

## 6. Depth & Elevation

| Level | Treatment | Use |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| 0 | Flat, textured | Background layers, paper textures |
| 1 | Soft shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(45,90,61,0.08)) | Cards, small containers |
| 2 | Medium shadow (0 4px 16px rgba(45,90,61,0.12)) | Elevated cards, character containers |
| 3 | Glowing shadow (0 0 20px rgba(244,208,63,0.3)) | Magic elements, special items |
| 4 | Layered depth | Background/foreground scene composition |

**Shadow Philosophy:** Shadows are soft, warm, and colored (green/brown tint) rather than harsh gray. Magic elements have golden glows. Depth is created through atmospheric layering and soft edges, not sharp shadows.

---

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Create warm, inviting compositions
- Use soft edges and painterly textures
- Include charming character illustrations
- Add magical decorative touches (stars, sparkles, flowers)
- Maintain storybook narrative feel
- Use layered depth with foreground/background elements
- Keep colors warm and soft

### Don't
- Use harsh geometric shapes
- Create dark or intimidating imagery
- Add photorealistic elements
- Use cold color palettes
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos
- Use sharp corners or rigid grids
- Create cluttered or busy layouts

---

## 8. Responsive Behavior

| Breakpoint | Width | Behavior |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Mobile | < 640px | Single column, headline 32px, stacked illustrations |
| Tablet | 640-1024px | 2-column layout, headline 40px, maintained layering |
| Desktop | > 1024px | Full layered layout, headline 48-64px, panoramic compositions |

**Touch Targets:** Minimum 48px for interactive elements

**Collapsing Strategy:**
- Layered compositions simplify to stacked elements
- Side-by-side character + text stacks vertically
- Decorative elements reduce in number but remain
- Panoramic scenes crop to focus on key elements

**Image Behavior:**
- Illustrations maintain soft edges at all sizes
- Background textures scale seamlessly
- Character illustrations remain prominent
- Decorative elements may hide on very small screens

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

### Quick Color Reference
- Background: #E8F4FC (Soft Sky Blue) / #FFF8E7 (Warm Cream)
- Primary Text: #2D5A3D (Deep Forest)
- Accent: #F4D03F (Golden Yellow), #E8A0BF (Rose Pink)
- Nature: #87A96B (Sage Green)

### Example Component Prompts

1. **Storybook Hero:** "Soft Sky Blue (#E8F4FC) background with a central friendly character illustration. Warm Cream (#FFF8E7) content area below with story title in whimsical serif (48px, Deep Forest). Decorative stars and sparkles around the character. Soft shadows."

2. **Magic Item Card:** "Rounded card (24px radius) with Warm Cream background. Golden border glow. Central illustration of a magical object. Title in whimsical font (24px, Golden Yellow). Description in rounded sans (16px, Warm Brown). Sparkle decorations in corners."

3. **Character Showcase:** "Layered composition with background scene (forest, sky). Foreground character with soft shadow. Name in whimsical serif (32px). Small companion creature nearby. Sage Green and Rose Pink decorative elements."

4. **Story Chapter Section:** "Warm Cream (#FFF8E7) background with decorative corner swirls. Chapter number in Golden Yellow (64px). Title in whimsical serif (32px, Deep Forest). Body text in rounded sans (16px, Warm Brown). Pull quote in Rose Pink italic."

5. **Enchanted Forest Scene:** "Panoramic horizontal composition. Layered background: soft sky blue gradient, distant trees in lighter green, foreground trees in Sage Green. Magical floating objects (books, orbs) in Golden Yellow glow. Small creatures hidden in the scene."

6. **Content Scroll:** "Parchment-colored container with rolled edge appearance. Ribbon decoration in Rose Pink. Content inside with Deep Forest headings and Warm Brown body text. Decorative leaf motifs in corners."

### Iteration Guide

1. **Start with atmosphere:** Establish the background color and texture first.
2. **Add focal character:** Place the main character or element as the visual anchor.
3. **Layer depth:** Add foreground and background elements for dimensional richness.
4. **Magical touches:** Add sparkles, glows, and decorative elements last.
5. **Soften everything:** Ensure edges are soft, colors are warm, and nothing feels harsh.
6. **Maintain warmth:** If a color feels cold or clinical, warm it up toward cream/yellow.
