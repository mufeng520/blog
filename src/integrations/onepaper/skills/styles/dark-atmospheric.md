# Dark Atmospheric Design System

Dark moody aesthetic with deep colors and glowing accent elements.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

- **Brand Personality:** Mysterious, sophisticated, dramatic, cinematic
- **Emotional Tone:** Intense, immersive, premium, awe-inspiring
- **Key Characteristics:**
  - Cinematic dark mode aesthetic with atmospheric depth
  - Deep purples, blacks, and rich shadows with glowing accents
  - Dramatic visual contrast creates visual impact
  - Mysterious and sophisticated atmosphere
  - Perfect for evening events, creative industries, and premium brand presentations

## 3. Typography Rules

**Font Families:**
- **Headlines:** Elegant serif or refined sans-serif (e.g., "Playfair Display", "Cinzel", or "SF Pro Display")
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (e.g., "Inter", "Roboto", or "SF Pro Text")

| Role | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height | Letter Spacing | Notes |
|------|------|------|--------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| H1 (Hero Title) | Elegant Serif | 64px | Light (300) | 1.1 | 0.02em | High contrast against dark background, may have subtle glow |
| H2 (Section Title) | Elegant Serif | 42px | Regular (400) | 1.2 | 0.01em | Medium to bold weight for presence |
| H3 (Subsection) | Refined Sans | 28px | Medium (500) | 1.3 | 0 | Clean, modern contrast to serif headlines |
| Body | Clean Sans | 18px | Regular (400) | 1.7 | 0 | Readable against dark backgrounds |
| Caption | Clean Sans | 14px | Regular (400) | 1.5 | 0.01em | Light Gray for metadata |
| Data / Number | Refined Sans | 48px | Bold (700) | 1.0 | -0.02em | Glowing effect optional for key metrics |

**Principles:**
- Light/white text for maximum contrast on dark backgrounds
- Elegant serif for headlines adds sophistication
- Clean sans-serif for body ensures readability
- Generous line height (1.7) for comfortable reading in dark mode
- Subtle glow effects on key headlines for dramatic emphasis

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Transparent background, Electric Purple (#8B5CF6) text, 1px Electric Purple border with glow, 4px border-radius
- **Secondary:** Transparent background, Cyan Blue (#06B6D4) text, 1px Cyan Blue border
- **Glow:** Electric Purple background with white text, subtle purple glow effect, 4px border-radius
- All buttons may have subtle glow on hover

### Cards & Containers
- Rich Navy (#1A1A2E) background
- 1px Dark Gray (#2D3748) border
- 8px border-radius
- Subtle inner glow or gradient for depth
- No harsh shadows — depth comes from glow and gradients

### Inputs & Forms
- Rich Navy (#1A1A2E) background
- 1px Dark Gray border
- 4px border-radius
- Pure White text
- Focus state: Electric Purple glow border

### Navigation
- Horizontal nav with transparent or Rich Navy background
- Active item: Electric Purple text with glow underline
- Inactive items: Light Gray text
- Subtle gradient or glow on hover

### Image Treatment
- Silhouettes with backlit edges
- Dark, moody photography with high contrast
- Images may have gradient overlays for text legibility
- Neon-style highlights on key image elements

### Distinctive Components
- **Glowing Elements:** Accent elements and borders with subtle glow
- **Gradient Backgrounds:** Subtle gradient from darker edges to slightly lighter center
- **Atmospheric Effects:** Fog or particle effects for depth
- **Neon Highlights:** Key elements with neon-style glow
- **Audio Waveforms:** Sound visualizations for entertainment content
- **Radiating Light:** Circles and orbs with light rays
- **Cinematic Letterboxing:** Optional black bars for film aesthetic

## 5. Layout Principles

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 8px
- Margins: 10-12% on all sides
- Gap between elements: 24px (small), 40px (medium), 64px (large)
- Dramatic spacing for cinematic feel

### Grid & Container
- Flexible grid with dramatic focal points
- Elements positioned to create visual drama
- Asymmetric layouts preferred for dynamic feel
- Containers may have gradient backgrounds

### Whitespace Philosophy
- Dark space is atmospheric, not empty
- Whitespace creates mystery and focus
- Dramatic contrast between dense content areas and open dark space
- Use darkness as an active design element

### Border Radius Scale
- Small: 4px (buttons, inputs)
- Medium: 8px (cards, containers)
- Large: 16px (modals, featured sections)
- Circular: 50% (glowing orbs, avatar highlights)

## 6. Depth & Elevation

| Level | Treatment | Use |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| 0 (Flat) | No glow, Dark Gray border | Inactive elements |
| 1 (Subtle Glow) | 1px accent border with soft glow | Interactive elements, hover states |
| 2 (Elevated Glow) | Accent-colored glow (4-8px spread) | Active buttons, key highlights |
| 3 (Radiant) | Strong glow + gradient background | Featured elements, hero sections |

**Shadow Philosophy:** Dark atmospheric style inverts traditional shadow thinking. Instead of dark shadows on light backgrounds, depth is created through light glows on dark backgrounds. Glow effects are the primary elevation tool — they simulate light sources in a dark environment. Gradients from dark edges to lighter centers create atmospheric depth. Use glow sparingly — when everything glows, nothing stands out.

---

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Maintain high contrast for readability (white text on dark backgrounds)
- Use glowing effects sparingly for emphasis
- Create atmospheric depth with gradients
- Design dramatic visual focal points
- Keep text crisp against dark backgrounds
- Use dark space intentionally to create mystery
- Apply accent colors as glows rather than solid fills
- Create cinematic compositions with dramatic lighting

### Don't
- Overuse neon effects — less is more
- Create low-contrast text combinations (avoid gray-on-gray)
- Use bright or light backgrounds
- Add cluttered busy elements
- Apply glow to every element
- Use more than 2-3 glow colors in one view
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos
- Create flat, uniform dark backgrounds without depth

---

## 8. Responsive Behavior

| Breakpoint | Width | Behavior |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Desktop | >= 1280px | Full cinematic layouts, multiple glow effects, atmospheric depth |
| Tablet | 768px - 1279px | Reduced glow intensity, simplified atmospheric effects |
| Mobile | < 768px | Single column, minimal glow effects, larger touch targets |

**Touch Targets:** Minimum 44x44px for interactive elements

**Collapsing Strategy:**
- Side-by-side content stacks vertically
- Complex atmospheric effects simplify on smaller screens
- Glow effects reduce in intensity to avoid overwhelming small screens
- Maintain dark background and high contrast at all sizes

**Image Behavior:**
- Dark, moody images scale proportionally
- Gradient overlays maintained for text legibility
- Silhouette effects preserved at all sizes

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

### Quick Color Reference
- Background: #0D0D1A (Deep Purple-Black)
- Alt Background: #1A1A2E (Rich Navy)
- Primary Text: #FFFFFF (Pure White)
- Secondary Text: #A0AEC0 (Light Gray)
- Primary Glow: #8B5CF6 (Electric Purple)
- Secondary Glow: #06B6D4 (Cyan Blue)
- Tertiary Accent: #EC4899 (Magenta Pink)

### Example Component Prompts

1. **Cinematic Title Slide:** "Create a title slide with Deep Purple-Black (#0D0D1A) background. Title in large elegant serif (Pure White #FFFFFF) with subtle Electric Purple (#8B5CF6) glow. Subtle gradient from darker edges to slightly lighter center. A small radiating light orb in Electric Purple near the title. Atmospheric fog effect at the bottom."

2. **Glowing Metric Card:** "Design a metric card with Rich Navy (#1A1A2E) background and 1px Dark Gray border. Large metric number in Pure White with subtle Cyan Blue (#06B6D4) glow. Label in Light Gray below. 8px border-radius. On hover, border glows Cyan Blue."

3. **Neon Accent Button:** "Create a primary button with transparent background, Electric Purple (#8B5CF6) text, and 1px Electric Purple border. Subtle purple glow around the border. On hover: Electric Purple background with white text, stronger glow. 4px border-radius."

4. **Audio Waveform Visualization:** "Design an audio waveform display on dark background. Waveform bars in gradient from Cyan Blue (#06B6D4) to Electric Purple (#8B5CF6). Bars have subtle glow. Background: Deep Purple-Black with atmospheric gradient. Peak bars glow brighter in Magenta Pink (#EC4899)."

5. **Feature Highlight Section:** "Create a feature highlight with a glowing accent icon (Electric Purple #8B5CF6 with glow effect) on the left. Title in Pure White elegant serif. Description in Light Gray. Background gradient from Rich Navy (#1A1A2E) to Deep Purple-Black (#0D0D1A). Subtle particle effect in background."

6. **Cinematic Timeline:** "Design a vertical timeline on dark background. Timeline line in Dark Gray (#2D3748) with glowing milestone dots in Amber (#F59E0B). Each milestone has a Rich Navy card with white title and Light Gray description. Cards have subtle inner glow. Atmospheric fog at edges."

### Iteration Guide
1. Start with Deep Purple-Black (#0D0D1A) background
2. Add atmospheric gradient from darker edges to lighter center
3. Add content with Pure White headlines and Light Gray body
4. Apply accent colors as glows, not solid fills
5. Add subtle atmospheric effects (fog, particles) for depth
6. Use glow effects sparingly — only on key emphasis elements
7. Ensure all text maintains high contrast against dark backgrounds
8. Review for clutter — dark atmospheric style needs breathing room
